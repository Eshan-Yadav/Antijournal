A Database of Immunodominant B cell Epitopes.The database is having 3031 peptide enteries.The peptides in the database has varying measure of immunogenicity.This database can assist in the development of method for predicting B cell epitopes, desigining synthetic vaccines and in disease diagnosis.

IEDB(The Immune Epitope Database and Analysis Resource)

Click here to visit the website

The IEDB contains data related to antibody and T cell epitopes for humans, non-human primates, rodents, and other animal species.

AntiJen Database

Click here to visit the website

AntiJen v2.0, is a database containing quantitative binding data for peptides binding to MHC Ligand, TCR-MHC Complexes, T Cell Epitope, TAP , B Cell Epitope molecules and immunological Protein-Protein interactions. Most recently, AntiJen has included Peptide Library, Copy Numbers and Diffusion Coefficient data.

MHCBN

Click here to visit the website

The MHCBN is a curated database consisting of detailed information about Major Histocompatibility Complex (MHC) Binding,Non-binding peptides and T-cell epitopes.The version 4.0 of database provides information about peptides interacting with TAP and MHC linked autoimmune diseases.

UniProtKB

Click here to visit the website

UniProt is a freely accessible database of protein sequence and functional information, many entries being derived from genome sequencing projects. It contains a large amount of information about the biological function of proteins derived from the research literature.
